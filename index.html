About Us

Welcome to our family! We are a family-run, ethical breeder specializing in Yorkshire Terriers and Maltese puppies. Each puppy is raised in our home, socialized, vet-checked, and given age-appropriate vaccinations. Our mission is to place healthy, well-tempered puppies into loving, responsible homes and to support adopters for life. Thank you for considering us!

Our Promise
  • Health & vaccinations before adoption
  • Early socialization and basic training
  • Clear health records and microchipping options
  • Lifetime support & guidance
